All About Sweet Potatoes

The best vegetable there is

The sweet potato is a starchy, large root vagetable with a sweet taste. They are known to contain a relatively high nutritional value, most notably the vitamin beta-varotene. Sweet potatoes have versalite uses for cooking. They can be baked, steamed, fried, microwaved. They are frequently used in savory dishes, curries, soups, burgers, desserts, and much more.

Fun Facts

  • Sweet potatoes are only distantly related to potatoes
  • The flesh of sweet potatoes can vary in color to include white, red, pink and purple
  • It is a misconception that orange sweet potatoes are the same as yam
